Tax

A Tax is a fee (generally in financial/monetory form) charged by any government for public works providing the best facilities and infrastructure to the nation. Such as building roads, maintaining public services (i.e. buses, railway, metro, offices, hospitals, etc).

The fund collected by government as Tax is used to fund different public expenditure programs.


Why Pay Tax ?

One has to pay taxes if he/she is earning, having a proper income. One is eligible to pay taxes only if a person has minimum income (a minimum amount is defined after which a tax will be charged) that could enable his/her survival.

Being a people of this nation, if we don't support our nation where we live in then who will support? It is a shameful act for not paying taxes. This means you're not participating and contributing for the country welfare. This is considered as tax stealing, thus it will invite serious punishment laws defined by constituion.


Income Tax

According to the Income Tax Act, 1961, every salaried person needs to pay an amount from their salary as tax to the country. This amount of tax is called the Income Tax.

The law consists of a lot of amendments and variations with subsections describing the details about tax payment, deductions, and computations. A lot of deductions from subsection 80C to 80U are available. The final amount after subtracting all the available tax-saving provisions and deductions is given to the government as the income tax on salary.
